Available Scholarships

Supporting your academic journey

Merit-Based Scholarship

Awarded to top-performing students with CGPA above 9.0. Covers up to 75% of tuition fees. Renewable annually based on academic performance.

Need-Based Assistance

Financial support for students from economically weaker sections. Requires income proof and academic records. Covers partial to full tuition fees.

Government E-Kalyan / NSP

Apply through the National Scholarship Portal or state platforms (e.g., Gujarat E-Kalyan). Includes SC/ST/OBC and minority scholarships.

Women in Tech

Special scholarship for female students enrolled in engineering & technology programs, promoting gender diversity in STEM.

How to Apply

  • Login to your student portal and navigate to the "Scholarships" section.
  • Choose the scholarship you’re eligible for.
  • Upload required documents: mark sheets, income proof, ID, etc.
  • Submit the form and track the application status online.
